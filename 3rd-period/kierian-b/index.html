ABOUT ME: MY PROGRESSION AS A PROGRAMMER

As a person I've actually always been vaguely interested in learning programming probably because younger me gaslit myself into wanting to become an engineer of sorts. So when I heard about the popularity of computer engineering I decided to just set my heart on computer engineering and programming. So that led me to eventually taking AP COMP SCI in junior year highschool, though I did have a little but of experience with HTML from some coding classes I took in elementary school. Though AP COMP SCI is where I definetely learned everything I knew now. It took many sleepless nights to design some of the projects I ended up working on, and many showers where I just sat there thinking about how I could make my projects better, but overall I can say that my experience with programming was overall positive and I can say I learned a lot about programming throughout the course of the year.
